Discovered in 1930, **Mbosi** meteorite has been known for centuries by locals, who call it Kimwondo. It was named  after the Mbozi district in Mbeya, Tanzania.

**Type:** Ungrouped iron and silicates<br>
**Weight:** 12.5 tons <br>
**Year Founded:** 1930
